Required permissions for cloud storage services

Here's why OWC requests certain permissions for using cloud storage services.

Microsoft OneDrive

Permission Explanation
Read your profile Essential permission to access your OneDrive. OWC does not read your user name, e-mail or any other profile information.
Open OneDrive files For loading warbands stored in files on your OneDrive. OWC does access only files that you select.
Have full access to all files you have access to For storing warbands on your OneDrive and also replacing existing warband files. OWC does not delete any files. If a <warbandname>.owc.txt-file exists in the folder you select, it will be replaced by the current warband with the same name.

Google Drive

Permission Explanation
Associate you with your personal info on Google Essential permission to access your Google Drive. OWC does not read your user name, e-mail or any other profile information.
See, edit, create, and delete only the specific Google Drive files you use with this app For loading, storing and replacing existing warband files on your Google Drive. OWC does access only files that you select. If a <warbandname>.owc.txt-file exists in the folder you select, it will be replaced by the current warband with the same name. OWC does not delete any files.
