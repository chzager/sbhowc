Terms of Service for Online Warband Calculator

Online Warband Calculator ("OWC", formerly "Online Warband Creator") is a tool for designing units and calculating warband points for the fantasy tabletop rule system "Song of Blades and Heroes" by Andrea Sfiligoi. It is for private use only. Usage is free of charge.
